Privacy Policy

Barn Office Organizer · published by Calx Software

Your data never leaves your device

Barn Office Organizer is a local-first desktop application. It runs entirely on your own Windows computer. It has no server, no cloud account, and no online component. Everything you enter stays on your machine.

What information the app stores

The app stores the records you create — boarders, horses, fields, contacts, feed, billing, calendar events, employee and payroll details, and any documents or photos you attach. All of it is saved in a database file and an attachments folder in your personal Windows app-data folder on your own computer.

What we collect

Nothing. Barn Office Organizer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your information. Specifically, the app contains:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No telemetry or crash reporting
  • No advertising or third-party trackers
  • No user accounts, sign-in, or cloud sync

Backups

Backups are optional and stay entirely under your control. When you enable backups or choose “Backup now,” the app writes a backup file to a folder you select on your own computer. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

When the app contacts the internet

The app does not send your data over the internet. The only outward actions are ones you explicitly trigger — for example, choosing to send feedback opens a new message in your own email program, which you control and send yourself. The app does not send email on your behalf.

Children’s privacy

Because the app collects no information and has no online services, it does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy
