# Barn Office Organizer

**Barn Office Organizer** is a local-first Windows desktop app for running a horse boarding
farm — boarders, horses, pastures, vet/farrier contacts, feed, a calendar of vet/farrier/lesson
events, document & photo attachments, billing, employees & payroll, and automatic backups — all
in one place, with a clean, keyboard-friendly interface.

Published by **Calx Software**.

## Your data stays on your machine

Barn Office Organizer runs entirely on your own computer. There is no server, no cloud account,
and no online sync. Everything you enter is stored locally on your device, and nothing is
collected, transmitted, or shared.

## Features

- **Boarders & horses** — full records with inherited vet/farrier/feed defaults
- **Fields & contacts** — pasture assignments, vet/farrier directory
- **Feeding** — priced feed options, per-horse amounts, a printable feeding schedule
- **Calendar** — vet/farrier visits, lessons, and recurring series with retrieval lists
- **Lessons** — instructors, scheduling, and prepaid lesson packages
- **Billing** — monthly invoices, payments, and carryover
- **Employees & payroll** — work shifts, pay schedules, and pay statements
- **Documents & photos** — attachments for horses and boarders
- **Backups** — one-click and automatic local backups you control

## Availability

Barn Office Organizer is a Windows desktop application, distributed through the
**Microsoft Store**.
